    Ranjana Script also known as Lantsa or kutila is an writing system developed in the 11th century. It consists of 36 consonants, 16 vowels and 10 numerals. This script is used to write headlines in Newspaper, printing "high status" documents (wedding invitations, certificates, etc) in Nepal in the Newar Language
